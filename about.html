About Me – Handmade by Hannah

Hi, I’m Hannah, the owner of Handmade by Hannah, a small cake studio based in Leicestershire.

I’ve been creating wedding and celebration cakes since 2008, turning a lifelong love of baking into a business I truly adore.

I work from my custom-built cake kitchen and serve customers across the East Midlands, including Leicester, Hinckley, Market Bosworth, and beyond.

With over 15 years of experience in the wedding industry, I take pride in designing cakes that not only look beautiful but taste amazing too.

Every cake I make is crafted with care, and I personally guide each customer through the process—from the first design ideas to delivery or collection.

Because I run the business myself, I’m able to offer a completely personal and friendly service.

I also understand how important it is to cater to different dietary needs, so I offer gluten-free, dairy-free, egg-free, nut-free, and vegan options to ensure everyone can enjoy a slice.
